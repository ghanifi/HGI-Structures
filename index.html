Welcome to HGI Structures

HGI Structures provide a full range of professional services in the field of structural engineering and project management for both new build and refurbishment projects.

We specialise in structural engineering and repair for masonry, steel and concrete structures — working closely with Chartered Surveyors, Architects and Town Planning consultants to offer a complete service for residential and commercial clients.

In addition, we provide a complete design and construction service starting with planning and building regulations through to project management and completion.
