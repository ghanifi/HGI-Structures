What We Do

Our Services

HGI Structures provide a full range of professional services in structural engineering and project management for both new build and refurbishment projects. We specialise in structural engineering and repair for masonry, steel and concrete structures. Our expertise includes substructure design, basements, renovations and extensions, loft conversions, temporary work design, steel connection design and fabrication drawings, building defects and repair solutions, building regulations and project management.

In addition, we provide a complete design and construction service starting with planning and building regulations through to project management and completion. We work with a wide range of projects and provide services to both residential and commercial buildings, aiming to offer a personal and professional service that fulfils our clients' building and development needs.

Full Service Offering

Basements

We design substructure solutions including basement excavations and retaining walls for residential and commercial properties. Full waterproofing strategy included.

Structural Design & Reports

Structural calculations, engineering reports and construction drawings for masonry, steel and concrete structures across all building types and scales.

Renovations & Extensions

Structural engineering support for side, rear and wrap-around extensions, internal structural alterations, and full refurbishment projects.

Loft Conversions

Structural design for loft conversions including dormer windows, hip-to-gable conversions, and full roof reconstruction with steel frame.

Temporary Works Design

Design of temporary structures required during construction — including temporary propping systems, falsework, and shoring to ensure safe site operations.

Steel Connections Design

Detailed design and fabrication drawings for all steel connection types — bolted, welded, and moment connections — to fabricator-ready standard.

Building Defects & Repairs

Investigation, assessment and repair design for structural defects including cracking, subsidence, corrosion and deterioration in masonry and concrete.

Building Regulations

Preparation and submission of Building Regulations structural packages, coordination with Building Control, and compliance sign-off for all project types.

Project Management

End-to-end project management from initial planning and procurement through to on-site supervision and final completion — coordinating all design disciplines.
